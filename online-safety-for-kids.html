Why Online Safety is Crucial for Today's Youth

At iThink Academy, we believe that online safety education is essential for helping youth make informed decisions, protect themselves, and thrive online. This course equips young individuals with the tools to maintain privacy, recognize dangerous situations, and foster healthy online behaviors.

Throughout this course, students will learn how to safeguard personal information, understand the impact of digital footprints, identify online risks, and develop skills to act responsibly in both social and professional online settings.

Understanding Privacy Online

This lesson provides a comprehensive overview of how to protect personal information across various platforms such as social media, gaming environments, and websites. Students will learn to set privacy controls and understand the potential consequences of oversharing online.

Cyberbullying and Online Etiquette

In this section, students will explore the issue of cyberbullying, how to recognize harmful behavior, and strategies to address it. The course also emphasizes maintaining respectful and positive online interactions through the principles of digital etiquette.

Identifying Online Scams and Fraud

This module equips students with the knowledge to detect fraudulent schemes and online scams. Students will learn practical tips on how to avoid common traps such as phishing emails, fake job listings, and fraudulent websites.

Building a Positive Digital Footprint

In this lesson, students will explore the importance of cultivating a positive online reputation. Through case studies and interactive activities, learners will gain insights into how digital footprints can affect their future education and career prospects.
